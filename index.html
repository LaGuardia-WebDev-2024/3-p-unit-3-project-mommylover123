Recipe #1

  • Time: 5-10mins
  • Serves: as much as you want
Ingredient list: day old rice, chili, green onion, egg, flank steak, dark soy sauce
Ingredients Quantity
whatever you feel like

Step 1: crack # of eggs into a bowl and beat it and cut your other ingredients

Step 2: turn on your stove to high heat and scramble your eggs until fluffy

Source: Some recipes websites

Step 3: get your chopped up ingredients(leave half of your chopped spring onion as garnish) and put it into your pan

Step 4: stir fry it and add your day old rice

Step 5: mix it around and add your sauces and continue mixing Step 6: add your eggs and mix it; turn off the fire and plate up( add your leftover spring onions on top)
